DESCRIPTION  (max 4000 chars)
----------------------------------------------------------------
ShopMind is the grocery list that thinks ahead.

Build one master list and ShopMind remembers it forever — then it does the
hard part for you: the moment you walk near a store that actually carries
items on your list, you get a smart heads-up naming exactly what to grab.

Designed to remove friction for busy and ADHD brains, ShopMind turns the
chaos of shopping into a calm, guided experience.

SMART STORE ALERTS
• Get notified when you're near a store carrying items you need — even with
  the app closed.
• Alerts name the specific items that store is likely to have.

A LIST THAT WORKS THE WAY YOU DO
• A persistent running list — items never vanish, just check them off.
• Mark items urgent so they float to the top.
• Group by aisle or keep it flat.
• Swipe to delete, reorder in seconds, and a live notification widget.

ADD ITEMS EFFORTLESSLY
• Scan a barcode to add a product instantly.
• Paste a recipe and ShopMind extracts the ingredients automatically.
• Ask the built-in AI assistant: "ingredients for tacos for 4" — done.
• One-tap templates for common shops.

FIND THE BEST STORE
• See nearby stores ranked by how much of your list they cover.
• Plan an optimal multi-stop route across several stores.
• One tap for turn-by-turn navigation.

KNOW YOUR HABITS
• Track spending per trip and per store.
• See which store is cheapest for each item.
• Trip history, streaks, and "running low" reminders for things you rebuy.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No account required. Your lists and history live on your device.
• We don't run servers that store your data.

ShopMind is a subscription app billed at $9.99/month. Subscriptions auto-renew
unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends; manage or cancel in
your device's account settings.

APP PRIVACY  ("nutrition labels" under App Privacy)
================================================================
ShopMind does not operate servers that collect user data. Recommended answers:

• Do you collect data from this app?  ->  You can answer "No, we do not
  collect data" IF you add no analytics/crash SDKs. Everything is stored
  on-device, and location/AI/barcode calls go directly to third parties
  (Google, Open Food Facts) without passing through your servers.

• If Apple's reviewer asks about location: it is used ONLY for app
  functionality (finding nearby stores / proximity alerts), processed on
  device, not linked to identity, NOT used for tracking.

• Camera: used only to read barcodes in real time; no images stored or sent.

If you LATER add analytics (e.g., Firebase, Sentry), you must update these
answers to declare what those SDKs collect.

REVIEW NOTES  (paste into "Notes" for the reviewer)
================================================================
ShopMind is a grocery-list app. Key points for review:

1. BACKGROUND LOCATION: Used to alert the user when they pass near a grocery
   store that carries items on their list, even when the app is closed. This
   is a core, user-facing feature explained during onboarding and toggled on
   only with explicit opt-in in Settings > Background Tracking.

2. SUBSCRIPTION: The app requires a $9.99/month auto-renewable subscription.
   The paywall shows price, billing terms, and links to Terms of Use and
   Privacy Policy before purchase. A "Restore purchases" option is provided.

3. AI ASSISTANT: Optional. Uses the user's own free Google Gemini API key
   entered in Settings; requests go directly to Google.

No demo account is needed — the app has no login.
