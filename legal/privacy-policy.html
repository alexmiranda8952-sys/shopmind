ShopMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

ShopMind ("we", "the app") is a grocery-list app that helps you remember what to buy and alerts you when you're near a store carrying items on your list. We built ShopMind to keep your data on your device. This policy explains what we access and why.

The short version

  • We do not have accounts, and we do not run servers that store your data.
  • Your lists, trips, prices, and settings are stored only on your device.
  • Location is used on your device to detect nearby stores; it is never sent to us.
  • Optional features call third-party services (Google, Open Food Facts) directly from your device.

Information we access

Location

With your permission, ShopMind reads your device location to (a) show nearby grocery stores and (b) — if you enable background tracking — alert you when you pass near a store that carries items on your list. Location is processed on your device and sent only to Google Maps to look up nearby stores. We do not log, store, or transmit your location to any ShopMind-operated server.

Camera

If you use the barcode scanner, ShopMind accesses your camera solely to read product barcodes. Images are processed in real time and are not stored or transmitted.

Grocery data

Your lists, trip history, recorded prices, and preferences are saved in your device's local storage. They never leave your device unless you explicitly share a list, which produces a code you choose to send.

Third-party services

ShopMind connects directly from your device to the following services for specific features:

  • Google Maps Places API — to find grocery stores near your location. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API (optional AI assistant) — if you add your own API key, your typed requests are sent to Google to generate list suggestions. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open Food Facts — to look up product names from scanned barcodes. See their Privacy Policy.

We do not control these services and are not responsible for their data practices.

Purchases

ShopMind is offered as a paid subscription processed by the Apple App Store / Google Play Store. Payment and billing are handled entirely by the platform; we never see your payment details. Manage or cancel your subscription in your device's account settings.

Children

ShopMind is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Data deletion

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes all of it. There is nothing for us to delete on our end.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
